01   About

Engineer.
Researcher. Builder.

I hold a PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Victoria, where I developed novel deep learning architectures — including P-MIMO LSTM and CNN-LSTM networks — for WiFi indoor localization, achieving up to 50% performance improvement over existing methods.

At Fortinet, I work as a Staff Embedded Software Developer building enterprise security products including Fortigate, FortiSASE, GTP/SEPP for 5G networks, VoIP security, SSL VPN, antivirus, and IPS systems.

Prior to Fortinet, I implemented HF modems for tactical SDR radios at Viettel R&D and worked as a Field Applications Engineer at Avnet Asia, specializing in Xilinx FPGA (Zynq SoC) deployments.
